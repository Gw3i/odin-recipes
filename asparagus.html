Sauteed Garlic Asparagus

Description

A simple but tasty twist to regular old asparagus.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter or margarine
  • 1 bunch fresh asparagus
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped

Steps

  1. Melt butter or margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic and asparagus spears; cover and cook until asparagus is tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
