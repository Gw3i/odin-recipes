Spicy Beef Ramen

Description

One of the best dishes in the japanese cuisine, in my opinion. Soup on a whole different level.
Try it and enjoy the taste of Japan.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 ounce) package beef-flavored ramen noodles
  • 1 cup thinly sliced shiitake mushrooms
  • Â½ cup frozen shelled edamame
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Sriracha sauce, or more to taste
  • 1 (4 ounce) beef top sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste

Steps

  1. Cook ramen in a saucepan according to package directions, adding mushrooms, edamame, soy sauce, and sriracha in the last 2 minutes of cooking. Add steak in the last minute of cooking. Top servings with cilantro and, if desired, additional Sriracha sauce.
